Standard Curriculum

Kinder (3-6) Years

Establish a strong foundation through playful, hands-on activities and sensory exploration. Our program nurtures essential social skills, encourages curiosity, and builds the confidence needed for formal schooling.

Elementary School

Dive into core academics with a structured daily routine focused on literacy, math, and general knowledge. We foster independent thinking and build the essential study habits necessary for future success.

Middle (10-16) Years

Transition into deeper subject matter with a focus on scientific inquiry, critical analysis, and specialized studies. This stage empowers students to become independent learners and prepare for higher education.
